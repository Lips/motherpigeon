Mother Pigeon

I'm Tina Piña Trachtenburg and I make soft sculpture pigeons in New York City.

Help me 300 blah blah

First I'd like to thank everyone for all of your support. I started making pigeons because I love them so much. It has always upset me the amount of hatred there is for a beautiful little bird. I wanted to do something that would show people how much I love them. I started making them out of felt and wire. A soft sculpture of them. When I put them on the sidewalk for people to see, I noticed people would get excited and they really liked them. I could talk to them about how wonderful they are. I could share beautiful rescue stories. There are many people who return to tell me that they now see pigeons differently. I have many people come and tell me that they have bird feeders now. After three years of becoming motherpigeon I am able to support myself and all of the animals i feed on the support you have all given me. It is absolutely wonderful. Now I have always had the dream of doing an outstallation of 300 pigeons in the park. I feel that an oustallation this big will bring a huge awareness of the pigeon and their many extraordinary qualities.
